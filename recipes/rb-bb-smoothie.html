Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie

Description

The recipe to making this smoothie is as easy as this smoothie is delicious. You probably have all of what you need to make it but if not then do not fret, as the ingredients as very easily accessible.

As for it's nutritional content, this smoothie contains 195 calories, 5.8 grams of protein, 42.5 grams of carbs and 1.7 grams of fat

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 6 ounces Vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes into a blender. Blend until smooth.
